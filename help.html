#Sleeping Selfie

How Does Sleeping Selfie work?

  • Step 1: Press "have a go"
  • Step 2: Upload your #SleepingSelfie to twitter and copy the tweet's link, paste it in the box
  • Step 3: Nominate one of your friends and give their twitter username so they can do the same
  • Step 4: Go to the donate page and give one pound by texting POUND to 70011
  • Step 5: Thanks for giving to Comic relief, you are done!
