How Doees Sleepy Selfie Work?

Start by uploading your sleepy selfie to one of the social media sites listed. Next add the URL link for the Sleepy Selfie website. Nominate two other people and either tag them in your post or email them to let them know. Now donate using the text number provided. This will give money to Comic Relief. You are done! Thanks for suporting Comic Relief!
